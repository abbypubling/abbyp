        Behold! Big Chungus in Sonic Mania!
        Collect Carrots, Smash Badniks, And more in Big Chungus' 35th Game!
        All but 2 of Sonic's sprites seen GHZ have been replaced with custom sprites of Big Chungus by me. I have created all sprites Except the logos seen when launching the game and the title screen graphic of Big Chungus which were modified from images found on the internet.
        The crunch sound for collecting carrots is from the Totinos Mania mod. I'm confident including this sound isn't too big of a sin, as it's just a "crunch" sound.
